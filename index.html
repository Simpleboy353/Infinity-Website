Bittyconomy Features

Economy Features

With Economy, you have the best system ever designed! You can check the shop and buy items, then see your inventory using b!stats! You can even play the lottery! You also have the ability to bet x amount of money and have a chance to double it! Choose where to search when using the b!search command which will give you random spots where you can search!

Music Features

One of the high quality features the bot has is Music, you can customize the volume, make the bot stop the current song, delete a specific song in the queue and more!

Fun Commands

The Fun module includes even more modules in it! In which you have: Image Generation, Embed settings, and more!

Miscellaneous Commands

In the miscellaneous module, you'll find commands such as: Welcomer Settings, Prefix Settings, ChatBot Settings and even more! Get everything related to getting support with the bot and contacting the developers for any kind of issue!

Customisable Behaviour

You can customise how the bot will behave in your guild. Change the embed color, set custom guild specific Prefix and many more.
